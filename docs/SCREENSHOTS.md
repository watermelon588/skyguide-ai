## The observatory workspace

<img src="media/dashboard.png" alt="Dashboard" width="100%" />

> Observer location, telescope profile, live clock and conditions — with Astro
> docked on the right, answering questions grounded in your actual context.

<img src="media/tonights-brief.png" alt="Tonight's brief" width="100%" />

> **Tonight's Brief** — a written observing plan generated from the live sky.

<img src="media/top-targets.png" alt="Recommended targets" width="100%" />

> **Recommended for you** — ranked for your aperture, sky and history.

<img src="media/telescope-config.png" alt="Telescope configuration" width="100%" />

> Aperture, focal length, mount type and camera support — every number feeds
> the scoring engine.

<img src="media/moon.png" alt="Moon panel" width="100%" />

> Phase, illumination, rise/set and how much the Moon is spoiling tonight.

<img src="media/sky-quality.png" alt="Sky quality" width="100%" />

<img src="media/light-pollution.png" alt="Light pollution map and darker sites" width="100%" />

> Bortle-aware sky quality, plus darker sites within driving distance.

<img src="media/all-sky-chart.png" alt="All-sky chart" width="100%" />

> Everything above the horizon right now, plotted on one chart.

## Targets

<img src="media/target-detail.png" alt="Target panel — M13" width="100%" />

> The target panel: real imagery, catalogue identity, a 0–100 score, and live
> geometry for *your* coordinates — "above your horizon for 6.7 more hours."

<img src="media/explore-catalog.png" alt="Explore the catalog" width="100%" />

<img src="media/explore-catalog-2.png" alt="Catalog table" width="100%" />

> All 13,311 objects — Messier, NGC and IC — charted by type, brightness and
> constellation, then filterable down to the one you want.
